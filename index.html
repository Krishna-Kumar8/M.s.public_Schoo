Welcome to Pratik Central school

PRATIK CENTRAL SCHOOL was established in 2016 and it is managed by the Pvt. Unaided. It is located in Rural area. It is located in Jandha Road Pakuli Bidupur (R S) Vaishali district of Bihar. The school consists of Grades from 1 to 8. The school is Co-educational and it have an attached pre-primary section. The school is N/A in nature and isnot using school building as a shift-school. English is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.
This school has Private building. It has got 13 classrooms for instructional purposes. All the classrooms are in good condition. It has 2 othor rooms for non-teaching activities. The school has a separate room for Head master/Teacher. The school has Pucca boundary wall. The school has have electric connection. The source of Drinking Water in the school is Tap Water and it is functional. The school has 5 boys toilet and 2 girls toilet and it is functonal. The school has a playground. The school has a Library and 600 books in its library. The school does not need ramp for disabled children to access classrooms. The School has 4 Computers for teaching and learning purposes and all are functional.The school is having a computer aided learning lab. The school is not providing mid-day meal.

THE AIMS OF OUR SCHOOL

At Pratik Central School, we believe every child has the right to equality of opportunity and access to the whole curriculum. We aim to provide a learning environment for every child which:

  • is safe and secure, exciting, stimulating, challenging and relevant.
  • develops the confidence and talent of every individual.
  • fosters self esteem, self-awareness and motivates learners.
  • promotes the qualities of being resourceful, reflective and resilient, is appropriate and matched to individual needs.
  • offers a wide range of activities which includes the requirements of the National Curriculum.

RULES & REGULATIONS

    Mode Of Payment For Day-Scholars

  • Students are required to pay their school fee as per our schedule between 1st to 10th of the month without fine.
  • Payment of fee with a late fine of Rs. 50/- is allowed between 20th to 25th of the month.
  • Students who fail to pay their fee in the period specified above will have to pay it with an additional fine of Rs. 100/- for each of the consecutive periods.
  • The names of all such students, who fail to pay their fee continuously for three months, will be struck off from the register and if they seek re-admission they will have to pay Rs. 500/- as re-admission charge.

    Attendance

  • Students have to attend their classes regularly and punctually.
  • Students absent without leave must come with an application giving sufficient reason for the absence duly signed by the guardian.
  • As per CBSE by-laws at least 75% attendance in classes held is must for the students, failing which they will not be promoted to higher classes.
  • Students have to be present on the closing and the opening day of any vacation or holiday.

Fine For Absence

  • A day scholar shall be fined Rs. 5/- per day for absence without leave.
  • The names of students continuously or discontinuously absent for 7 days in a month without giving prior information will be struck off from the attendance register. Re-admission will be possible only on sufficient ground after consideration on payment of Rs. 500/- as the re-admission charge. Without re-admission the student will not be allowed in the class.

Examinations And Promotion

  • The school conducts unit tests and internal assessments during the whole session.
  • A student is promoted to the next class on the basis of his/her performance in these tests and assessments.
